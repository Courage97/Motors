Online Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order online?

You can place an order online by visiting our website and selecting the desired car model. Follow the prompts to customize your vehicle and proceed to the checkout process.

What payment methods are accepted for online purchases?

We accept various payment methods, including credit/debit cards, bank transfers, and online payment platforms such as PayPal.

Can I customize my car before ordering online?

Yes, you can customize your car with various options such as color, trim, features, and accessories during the online ordering process.

How long does it take for the car to be delivered after placing an order online?

Delivery times may vary depending on factors such as vehicle availability, customization options, and your location. Our team will provide you with an estimated delivery date at the time of purchase.

What should I do if I encounter any issues with my online order?

If you encounter any issues with your online order, please contact our customer support team immediately. We will work to resolve any issues and ensure your satisfaction.
